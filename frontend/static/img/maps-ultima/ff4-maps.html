Final Fantasy IV Maps

These maps show the locations of treasure chests, secret passages, and such. This is a temporary location until they are integrated into my full FF4 web site. I don't know when that will happen.

Key

Red
Unable to walk here.
Yellow
Treasure
White
Trigger tile without a trigger (slightly buggy at times)
Green
Return door
Magenta
Go door
Blue
Doesn't increment step counter (no encounters)
Cyan
Event trigger

In addition, trigger tiles never increment the step counter. NPCs that have full color stand still, while NPCs that look like ghosts walk around.
