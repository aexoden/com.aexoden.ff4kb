When entering Toroia the first time, walk as fast as possible to the next room until Edward's room where you can take extra steps. 
Optional steps in the other hand are allowed because they don't waste time.

Things to look at while walking:
- On first screen, 4 steps from the first frog on the right side. If this frog walks off screen (down, right) then ingore the last 2 steps from this frog.
- On second screen, all 3 steps from the old lady.
- On third screen, 3 steps from the frog on the top left corner.

During the third screen, you can hold down+left instead of individual directions just to give you time to focus on the frog.

During Edward's cutscene, press Ctrl+F and type direction using WASD keys.

During the black chocobo forect, make sure to wait for about 2 to 16 frames at any point.

This manipulation takes a lot of practice from my experience.
